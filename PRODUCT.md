# Kaalkine Portfolio

## Register

brand

## Users & Purpose

YouTube creators and channel managers evaluating thumbnail designers. They arrive skeptical, leave wanting to hire. The site must communicate craft, strategy, and premium quality in under 30 seconds.

## Brand Personality

Bold, graphic, restless, precise. Thumbnail design is pop culture at 1280×720 — the site should feel like that energy, not a generic freelancer template.

## Anti-references

- Generic dark-mode SaaS landing pages
- Editorial magazine typography (Fraunces, drop caps, ruled grids)
- Purple gradients, glassmorphism cards, identical icon+heading grids
- ike.design copy-paste (structure inspired, voice is Kaalkine's own)

## Strategic Principles

1. **Show the work first** — thumbnails are the product; imagery leads every key fold.
2. **Type as texture** — Alumni Sans Pinstripe at display scale is the signature; Albert Sans carries clarity.
3. **Committed color** — near-black canvas, one hot accent, no beige hedging.
4. **Conversion without cringe** — confident CTAs, no hustle-bro copy.

## Accessibility

WCAG AA contrast minimum. Reduced-motion fallbacks on all animation. Focus-visible on all interactive elements.
